About Me

As I delve deeper into computer science, I hope to gain a better understanding of the world around me. I'm taking things one day at a time and enjoying every moment of the ride.

Check out my blog if you're interested in my adventures in programming, writing, and gaming. It's a casual space where I reflect on my journey. Maybe something will resonate with you.
