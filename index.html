About Me

Hey there! I'm Jonas, and welcome to my website.

I'm a 19-year-old student from Germany with a passion for technology and learning. While I'm still figuring things out, I enjoy exploring various areas of computer science and technology.

In addition to my tech pursuits, I'm also learning Japanese and have an appreciation for anime and manga culture. It's been an interesting journey so far.

Interests

I'm fortunate to have a wide range of interests that keep me engaged and learning. Here are a few things that I'm currently exploring:

  • Programming: I enjoy making games and apps, and I'm always trying to learn new technologies.
  • Artificial Intelligence: I'm particularly fascinated by Natural Language Processing (NLP) and try to keep up with the latest developments in the field.
  • Physics and Electronics: While I'm not an expert, I find these subjects intriguing and try to stay informed about new developments.
  • Gaming: I enjoy playing games like Rocket League and osu!, and I sometimes try my hand at speedrunning.
  • Language Learning: I'm currently learning Japanese, which has been a rewarding challenge. As well as Spanish.

Personal Projects

I'm always working on various projects to apply what I've learned and explore new technologies. Here are a few projects I'm currently developing:

  • AniDesk: An AniList desktop client. This project allows me to work on creating user-friendly interfaces for accessing and managing anime and manga lists (goal is reaching feature parity with the website).
  • KanjiWizard: A Japanese dictionary that pulls from various sources. This combines my interest in Japanese language learning with programming, creating a tool that I hope will be useful for other language learners. I may or may not have switched the framework for this more than 4 times (and I wonder why my projects never get finished).
  • Teabag: An FPS framework in the Godot game engine. I'm extremely inspired by Source engine games, and I'm aiming to recreate some of their feel and functionality within the Godot environment (is harder than I expected).

These projects are currently in development and haven't been published yet.
